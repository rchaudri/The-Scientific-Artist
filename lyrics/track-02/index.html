AI Commentary

Technical identity

Rohan calls the microphone a tool, turning performance into an instrument of work. The line connects student life, craft, and engineering-like control: he is still in school, but already treating language as equipment.

Artistic lineage

The sleep/death sequence and the Frost reference place the track inside a larger literary and hip-hop tradition. He borrows from familiar cultural memory, then bends it into his own urgency.

Local authorship

The Fremont line grounds the flex in place. The track is not only self-mythology; it frames authenticity as something tied to a real hometown archive.
