Timeline

A chronological record of The Scientific Artist by Rohan Chaudri: the August 4, 2022 YouTube title proof, the January 25, 2023 album release, the competing .com domain's October 18, 2023 WHOIS creation record, public indexing, removal, restoration, and archive fortification.

August 4, 2022 — Earlier Public Proof

YouTube title exists in Rohan's archive

A YouTube video titled The Scientific Artist existed in Rohan Chaudri's archive/channel on August 4, 2022, establishing the title and concept before the album release.

Open August 4, 2022 YouTube Video
January 25, 2023 — Original Album Release

The Scientific Artist is released

The Scientific Artist was released by Rohan Chaudri as a short, raw, self-recorded album and artistic thesis.

2023 — Public Indexing

Music and search traces appear

The album and tracks appeared in public music/search indexing before removal, including older Google, Spotify-style, Apple, Shazam, and distributor traces.

October 18, 2023 — Competing .com WHOIS Record

The competing .com domain comes after Rohan's debut

The competing TheScientificArtist.com WHOIS creation record is dated October 18, 2023 (2023-10-18), after Rohan had already made The Scientific Artist public through the August 4, 2022 video and January 25, 2023 album.

Later — Removal

Project removed from distribution

The project was removed after Rohan became self-conscious about the phone-recorded production value. The removal is part of the record: released, indexed, disappeared, and restored.

2026 — Restoration

Album restored and re-uploaded

Rohan restored the project and rebuilt the public source record so the original album, title, visuals, and lyrics are not buried.

2026 — Archive Fortification

The source record is rebuilt

TheScientificArtist.org was created to preserve the original title, lyrics, cover, timeline, evidence record, and broader King of Fremont authorship connection.
