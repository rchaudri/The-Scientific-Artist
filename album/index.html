Release History

The Scientific Artist was originally released on January 25, 2023 as Rohan Chaudri's debut album and artistic thesis. The project was raw, self-recorded, and direct: a phone-recorded source document from the period when Rohan was forming the science-art identity that later became central to his archive.

The project was later removed, then restored to preserve the original record.
